Football

Football is one of the academy’s most competitive sports. Students train weekly in tactical drills, endurance exercises, and inter-faculty competitions. The academy organizes friendly matches and tournaments to improve teamwork and discipline. The academy’s football team is known as one of the most successful and disciplined teams in the region. Over the years, the team has won numerous competitions through hard work, dedication, and excellent coaching. The players train with modern equipment, including a fully equipped gym, professional football kits, training cones, fitness machines, and a well-maintained football pitch that helps improve their performance and teamwork. The school also takes pride in its impressive trophy room, which is filled with medals, plaques, and championship trophies that showcase the team’s long history of success and achievement. The football team continues to inspire students and bring pride to the entire school community.
