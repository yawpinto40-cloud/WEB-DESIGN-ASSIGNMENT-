About SRC Sports Academy

SRC Sports Academy is a university sports development initiative designed to nurture talent, encourage fitness, and build leadership through sports participation. The academy provides structured training, competitions, and mentorship opportunities for students and the local community.



Mission

To empower students and young athletes through discipline, teamwork, and sporting excellence.


Vision

To become a leading university sports academy recognized for excellence in athletics, talent development, and community engagement.


Values

Discipline, integrity, teamwork, respect, commitment, and excellence.


Funding Sources

The academy receives support from the SRC, university sports bodies, community sponsors, alumni contributions, and partnerships with local sporting organizations.
