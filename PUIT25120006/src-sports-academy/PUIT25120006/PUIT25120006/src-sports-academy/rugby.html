Rugby

Rugby training focuses on strength, teamwork, and strategic gameplay. Students participate in conditioning exercises, tackling drills, and university league competitions throughout the semester. The academy’s rugby team is widely respected for its strength, teamwork, and outstanding achievements in interschool competitions. Through consistent training and determination, the team has built a strong reputation as one of the best rugby teams in the area. The players have access to high-quality training equipment, including fitness and strength-training machines, protective gear, tackling pads, agility tools, and a spacious rugby field that supports both practice sessions and major matches. The school’s trophy room proudly displays the rugby team’s awards, medals, and championship trophies, reflecting years of hard work and success. With skilled coaches and passionate players, the rugby team continues to grow and make the school proud both on and off the field.
